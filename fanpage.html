November 1, 1996 – November 15, 2017

Gustav Elijah Åhr

Known professionally as Lil Peep (often stylized as LiL PEEP), was an American rapper, singer, songwriter and model.He was known for helping pioneer an emo revival style of hip hop and rock music. Lil Peep was a frequent user of drugs due to his mental health issues, which ultimately caused his death on November 15, 2017. The Pima County Office of the Medical Examiner certified the cause of death to be an accidental fentanyl–alprazolam overdose, the latter of which was branded as Xanax.
